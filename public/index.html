Vorpal - A Dungeon Master's Bag of Tools

Magic Item Shop

A Dungeon Master's tool developed for 5th Edition Dungeons & Dragons. Once you've generated your inventory, click on each item's name to find out more information about it.

How It Works:

Location determines the size of a shop's inventory. Wealth determines the highest level of rarity among items. Type determines the type of items provided in the shop. For a smorgasbord of items, choose "Trader" as your shop type.

Due to the rarity of items, some shops might not be possible to generate (e.g. a Weaponsmith at a Wealth level of "Poor"). If generating larger inventories on a mobile device, please scroll to the bottom of the page to view item information after clicking.
